Customer service

How can I track my order?
You will receive an email with a tracking link as soon as your order leaves our warehouse. Just double check to make sure it didn't land in your junk folder, as emails sometimes end up there by accident.

Can I cancel or modify my order after it has been placed?
If you need to cancel or modify your order, please contact our customer service team as soon as possible. If your poster has not yet been printed, we may be able to make changes or cancel the order.
How long will delivery take?
Shipping is normally 3-5 business days.

Do you ship to countries outside of Sweden?
No, at the moment we only ship within Sweden.

What is the cost of shipping?
Shipping is free!
Can I return my order?
Since our posters are personalized, we do not accept returns or offer refunds unless the product arrives damaged or defective. If this happens, please contact us within 7 days of receiving your poster to arrange a return or exchange.
What forms of payment do you accept?
We use the online payment processing platform Stripe. Click here to read more about this and what types of payments that are accepted.
